Core Features

Discover what makes Credily the perfect platform for building trusted connections

Anonymous Reviews from Mutuals

Users can leave private, honest feedback about others that is only visible to mutual connections. This creates a circle of trust where feedback comes from people within your extended network, ensuring relevancy and context for the information shared.

AI-Powered Sentiment Analysis

Our advanced AI technology analyzes reviews to generate objective sentiment scores. The system evaluates language patterns, context, and tone to create an unbiased assessment of feedback, providing users with clear insights beyond individual opinions.

Smart Reputation Score

Our dynamic rating system evolves based on feedback patterns and engagement. Unlike static reviews, Credily's reputation score adapts to recent behavior, giving more weight to consistent feedback and creating a holistic picture of how someone is perceived by peers.

Category-Based Reviews

Move beyond generic ratings with our multi-dimensional review system. Users can provide feedback on specific characteristics like trustworthiness, honesty, kindness, and reliability, creating a more nuanced understanding of a person's social interactions and strengths.

Privacy & Moderation

We prioritize user wellbeing with robust privacy controls and AI-powered moderation. Our system automatically filters harmful, biased, or inappropriate content, while users can report unfair feedback for human review, creating a safe and respectful environment.

Mutual-Based Visibility

Our unique visibility model ensures reviews are only visible to those within a shared network. This creates context-aware reputation data that keeps feedback relevant to specific social circles, protecting privacy while still providing valuable insights for making connection decisions.
